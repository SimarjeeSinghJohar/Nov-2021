About Us

In the grocery store you will find fresh home baking, plus many more items, locally sourced when possible. You must try our cheese bread and one of our six flavours of butter tarts.

In the produce section you will find lots of fresh, locally grown items of special interest will be high-tunnel tomatoes grown just north of Harriston, Ontario, Canada.

In the Food-Booth you can enjoy a soft ice-cream cone or choose from a variety of eight different flavours of hard scooped ice-cream. If your are really hungry, you could get a hot breakfast sandwich (served all day) or try a soup or salad. Please ask about our Saturday dinner menu.

Thank you for visiting and enjoy your visit!
