Welcome to Misty Meadows Market Inc.

Located in the village of Conn, 15 minutes north of Arthur & 10 minutes east of Mount Forest. You will discover a quaint little country market bursting with a large variety of fresh fruits and vegetables in season, and year round home baking, groceries, crafts and gifts.

Fruits & Vegetables

Our Vegetables are locally grown in season. If they cannot be purchased locally, they are sourced from Elmira Produce Auction Co-op. Niagara Fruits are sold in season.

Meats & Cheeses

We source our meats and cheeses from local abattoir's. We stock Black Diamond, Ivanhoe, Black River, Salerno & St Albert Cheese.

Crafts & Gift-ware

Check out our rhythm, wall & mantle clocks, which sing hymns & gospel songs. Our China giftware is produced locally. Come by for your next gift!

Outdoor Furniture

See our line of hand-crafted outdoor Adirondack and Muskoka furniture. Fashioned from North American white cedar, our furniture is ideal for Summer relaxation, entertaining and family get-togethers.
